EXPERIENCE BEYOND UNKNOWN
The borderline between digital and physical reality becomes increasingly narrow. We are propelled into a new paradigm where those opposed environment are entangled. This is an attempt to reveal the process of creolization that our transforming society is living.

ELEGANCE FORGETS TIME

Kinetic Paradise by ECAL/Pablo Bellon & Jil Jander

Kinetic sculpture proposition for a cognac brand. Projects realised during a one week workshop with Media & Interaction Design and MAS in Design for Luxury & Craftsmanship.

DO NOT LEAN YOUR HEAD

UpsideDown by ECAL/Pablo Bellon

A multi device maze game of 5 levels. Use your phone to change gravity attraction and try to get to the goal. This project is the result of a course led at ECAL/Ecole cantonale d'art de Lausanne by Gaël Hugo

A KIDS PLAY?

Swinging Lights

Swinging lights is an interactive playground created for the 20th anniversary of la FASe (fondation genevoise pour l'animation socioculturelle) in collaboration with Jerome Gautier.

DO ROBOTS HAVE VACATION?

Lazy worker by ECAL/Pablo Bellon

Robots are taking our jobs, but what if robots also take our laziness at work? Lazy worker is a counterproductive robotic performance. It results from a one week workshop at ECAL with Thibault Brevet assisted by Marc Dubois.

WHERE IS THE DJ?

Casino

Vj and scenography at Casino de Montbenon, in collaboration with Hapax21 and La Main Mise. General overlook before the party starts.

FOR GOOD WRITERS ONLY

TypoRoll by ECAL/Pablo Bellon, Léonard Guyot & Maya Bellier

Typo Roll is a non-digital machine that can write any word of maximum five letters. We created a modular typography composed of three modules, each modules are used in minimum two different characters. Then we built this wooden sculpture with which we can interact and write our desired words. Project by ECAL/Léonard Guyot, Maya Bellier & Pablo Bellon This project is the result of a one week workshop led at ECAL/Ecole cantonale d'art de Lausanne by Angelo Benedetto and assisted by Pietro Alberti.

DO AI LOVE?

FlipChat by ECAL/Pablo Bellon

FlipChat shows the contrast of a conversation between two people. It is a completly automated visual traduction of a dialogue using javascript and text to speech api. This project is the result of a one week workshop led at ECAL/Ecole cantonale d'art de Lausanne by Cyril Diagne and assisted by Callum Ross.

EASY TO BREAK?

Distroy by ECAL/Pablo Bellon

A 3D animated triptic around unexpected destructions. This project is the result of a course led at ECAL/Ecole cantonale d'art de Lausanne by Thomas Traum

DISTURBANCE OR SATISFACTION?

POP! by ECAL/Pablo Bellon

A webtoy that gives you the satisfaction of exploting a balloon infinitly. This project is the result of a one week workshop led at ECAL/Ecole cantonale d'art de Lausanne by Mario Von Rickenbach and assisted by Tibor Udvari and Pietro Alberti.

Hi, my name is Pablo Bellon. I am an interaction designer and digital artist from Geneva, Switzerland. I am actually studying at ECAL(école d'art de Lausanne) in Bachelor Media and Interaction Design.

I spend the majority of my time creating audiovisual installations, content and plans for local events. I am particularly interested now in taking cultural elements linked to the digital world out of the screen by incarnating them into analogical or low-tech objects.

I take also part of the organisation of events especially with the collective "Bootleg" that I cofounded and the Hapax21 collective from Nyon.

If you're interested in collaborating with me, hire my service, or if you just want to have a discussion. Please contact me by email at pablo.bellon@protonmail.com or click on the social media links.

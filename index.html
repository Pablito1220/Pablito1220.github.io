Hi, my name is Pablo Bellon. I am an interaction designer and digital artist from Geneva, Switzerland. I am actually studying at ECAL(école d'art de Lausanne) in Bachelor Media and Interaction Design.

I am particularly interested in audiovisual installations, and I spend the majority of my time creating content and plans for parties or local exhibitions.

I take also part of the organisation of events especially with the collective "Bootleg" that I cofounded.

If you're interested in collaborating with me, hire me, or if you just want to have a discussion. Please contact me by email at pablo.bellon@protonmail.com or click on the social media links.
